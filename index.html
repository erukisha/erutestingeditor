🧳 What to Bring to Camp

Please make sure you bring the following items:

✅ What to Bring

  • 📖 Bible
  • 😄 GOOD attitude!!
  • 👖 Long pants
  • 🩳 Shorts – must come to at least the tip of your thumb when arms are down
  • 👕 T-shirts with God-pleasing words/pictures
  • 👚 Tank tops are ok – NO spaghetti straps; MODESTY is the goal!
  • 🛁 Towels and toiletries
  • 🧦 Undergarments and socks
  • 🧥 Jacket or sweatshirt; rain jacket if you can
  • 👟 Tennis shoes and flip flops
  • 🏊 Swimsuit – girls: one piece; boys: knee-length
  • 🛏️ Sleeping bag or bedding for a twin bed and pillow
  • ☀️ Sunscreen and sunglasses; hat if you want
  • 🚰 Water bottle
  • 🥾 OLD shoes and clothes that can get MUDDY
  • 📷 Camera
  • 🔦 Flashlight
  • 💊 Medications – give to the director at check-in (day-of-the-week sorter preferred; NOT allowed in cabins)

🚫 What to Leave at Home

  • 🍪 Snacks (we have a great cook — and no critters in cabins!)
  • 🎆 Fireworks
  • 📱 Cell phones and electronic devices
  • 💍 Jewelry or expensive toys, pens, etc.
  • 🔪 Weapons — including pocket knives
  • 💦 Squirt guns
  • 🐶 Pets
